ABOUT US

About Us – Mother’s Tawa

Mother's Tawa is a noble initiative taken by Pratima Jain, a Dubai based housewife, to support the wellbeing of those who do not have the time nor opportunity to prepare fresh, homely and hygenic Parathas and Theplas.

As a mother herself she realised the anguish that most mothers felt who sent their children to another city or county for further studies. "My children studied in Pune for 5 years and became huge junk addicts. They constantly complained about their hostel food and binged only on junk outside, resulting in deterioration of their health.', quotes Mrs. Jain.

She decided to move to India for a few months to nurture their health and back it into shape."During my stay in Pune, I found that most kids faced the same fate as my kids and were not lucky enough to have their parents live with them. I thus decided to stay here permanently and start Mother’s Tawa, which not only caters to children away from home, but to everyone looking for delicious & sumptuous tawa items, which many find relatively tedious to prepare.”
